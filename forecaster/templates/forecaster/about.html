🌿 What is ForecastIQ?

ForecastIQ is a Django web application that uses Prophet — a Machine Learning technique — to predict future values based on past patterns. It is designed for sales and revenue forecasting, but works with any time series data (stock prices, website traffic, temperature readings, etc.)

💡 Simple Explanation: You give the app your past sales numbers. It finds the trend (going up or down?), draws a line through your data, and extends that line into the future. Those extended values = your forecast!
